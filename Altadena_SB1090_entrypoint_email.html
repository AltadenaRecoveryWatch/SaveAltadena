Help us get SB 1090 right

Altadena is a tightly woven community, and a bill this consequential still has details to get right as it moves through Sacramento. We're gathering input now so SB 1090 can be amended to fit Altadena — massing and design of new SB 9 projects, helping displaced long-term residents return, and whether the bill's framework and 2030 sunset fit a recovery that won't be done by then.
