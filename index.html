STEP 2 · CALL THE GOVERNOR

Ask Gov. Newsom for an Executive Order

The fastest protection of all. The Governor extended SB 9 relief to fire-hazard zones in the burn areas by executive order (N-32-25) — but the hazard map left most of Altadena out. He can protect the entire Eaton Fire area with a stroke of the pen — and unlike SB 1090, an order can reach back to the speculative subdivisions already filed since the fire. Two minutes:

📞 Call the Governor: (916) 445-2841 ✉ Message the Governor's office →
What to say (30 seconds):
  • “I'm calling to ask Governor Newsom to protect Altadena's recovery from the Eaton Fire.”
  • Please grant an executive order protecting the Altadena / Eaton Fire disaster recovery area.
  • It would pause the by-right SB 9 / SB 1123 lot-splitting that lets out-of-town developers carve burned lots into up to ten units before families can even return.
  • A community still grieving and rebuilding shouldn't be the easiest place in the state to subdivide.
  • “Please act now. Thank you.”
