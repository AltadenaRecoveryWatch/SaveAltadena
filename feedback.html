Help us get SB 1090 right

SB 1090 is moving through the Legislature now — and there's still room to make it better. Tell us what matters most, and we'll use it to push for the right amendments.

Altadena is a tightly woven community, and a bill this consequential still has details to work through as it moves through Sacramento. We're gathering input now, while there's still time to amend SB 1090 so it truly fits Altadena — and your voice should help shape it.

Some of what we're working to get right in the bill

  • Massing & design. How new SB 9 projects fit the scale, look, and feel of our neighborhoods.
  • Bringing neighbors home. How to better facilitate the return of displaced long-term residents.
  • The bill's framework & timeline. Whether SB 1090's structure — and its proposed 2030 sunset — fit a recovery that won't be finished by then.
  • Anything else you think the bill should address.
