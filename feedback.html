Share your story

How is post-fire speculation affecting you and your neighbors? Your story puts real voices behind the fight to protect Altadena's recovery.

Out-of-town investors are buying burned Altadena lots and subdividing them under SB 1123 — turning single family lots into as many as ten units, by right, before survivors can even rebuild. We're collecting first-hand accounts of how this is affecting our community, to put real faces and stories behind SB 1090.

We'd love to hear about

  • A burned lot near you being bought up or carved into multiple units.
  • Being outbid or priced out trying to buy back or rebuild on your own block.
  • A renter or longtime neighbor who may not be able to return.
  • How the change in scale, traffic, or street safety would affect your neighborhood.
  • Anything else you're seeing or worried about.

Share your story below. With your permission, we may use it — anonymously if you prefer — in letters to legislators and in press outreach.
